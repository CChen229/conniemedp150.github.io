Above I created an image with photoshop by using mask layers. I took an image of my friend and masked it to hide the background. I then put another background image under it to create the city reflection. The sign and brick wall look was also created with masks. The reflection look through the sign was acheived by playing with the opacity levels of the masks. I created duplicated layers of the sign to create different levels of reflectivity on different areas of the sign.
